Bubble Tea and Karaoke

Are you the next singing sensation...or closer to the other end of the spectrum? Either way, come out for a fun night of karaoke and bubble tea with Inter-fellowship Association! Sing with your friends or have a solo moment in the spotlight; music and mics will be provided. And if you just want to go for the bubble tea and new friends, that works too. Hope to see (or hear) you there!

Doherty A302

Luau

Ho'olu komo la kaua! That's Hawaiian for "please join us," which we hope you'll do on Sunday night for an awesome luau! Party it up with Inter-fellowship Association and the best friends you're about to make. Music, food, drinks and more is just what you need to start your week off right. Don't worry, grass skirts are not required.

The Fence

Our furry friends are kindly provided by the Humane Society, which you can also learn more about at our social. Hope to see you there!

The Fence

LAN Party

IFA is hosting a LAN Party for the class of 2017! Calling all Summoners, Spartans, and Smashers to a fun and friendly night of video games, including (but not limited to) League of Legends, Halo, and Super Smash Bros! There will be prizes and FREE pizza to fuel your fight. Bring your laptop or console controller if possible. A $2 entry fee will go to a local charity. Come meet fellow gamers and bond over one of CMU's favorite pastimes!

Hamburg Hall 1000

Events Through The Year

Harvest

This is InterVarsity's regional fall conference, bringing together chapters from various campuses on a weekend retreat of rest, worship, and learning.

Official Website

Urbana

Urbana is a triennial event where over 25,000 people gather to worship God and hear how he is using people to accomplish his purposes throughout the world. For sixty years, God has been using Urbana conventions to change the lives of students and to advance His Kingdom in our world.

Official Website

MAC

Spanning two separate weekends, MAC (Ministering Across Cultures) is a resourceful training ground for students who desire to learn cross culture ministries and/or to relate to people of other ethnicities, background and culture. A great eye-opener and preparation especially for students who intend to go on short- or long-term missions and cross-cultural ministries.

Official Website

Cairo Global Urban Trek

Spend your summer living and serving among the poorest of the poor, under the leadership of national ministry partners. Trust God in hard places and follow Jesus to serve the least of these.

Official Website

Chapter Focus Week

Our regional CFW is held every spring at Cedar Campus, Michigan. It is a time where students in the chapter retreat from campus for a week of reflection, worship, learning, and planning for ministry efforts in the next school year.

Official Website

Veritas Forum

InterVarsity sponsors a local, annual event called Veritas Forum, a campus-wide event that encourages students and faculty to think more deeply about the difficult questions of life by inviting prestigious members of the community to discuss these questions in an open forum environment.
