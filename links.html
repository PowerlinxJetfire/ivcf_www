Churches

InterVarsity invites you to get connected to a local church! At InterVarsity, our members come from all sorts of backgrounds and cultures. As such, the churches that we attend are very diverse! From the formal to the casual, the big to the small, the modern to the traditional, and even from the early birds to the night owls, whatever sort of church you seek, our members will gladly help you find a church in which you can joyfully serve.

Fellowships @ CMU

InterVarsity at CMU is a part of CMU's Inter-Fellowship Association (IFA), which is the union of all the Christian fellowships on campus. The fellowships on campus are all united together to glorify God, but we all do it a little differently, so feel free to check out our fellow fellowships (no pun intended)!
