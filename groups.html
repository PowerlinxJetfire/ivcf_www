Large Group

Large group is a weekly event where all of the small groups gather in one place to worship God, know Him better, have fellowship with one another, and be ignited for the sake of our community groups. We meet in Doherty Hall 1217 from 4:30-6pm every Friday. Come and join us! We're excited to have you.

Small Groups

Most of InterVarsity's ministry happens in our small groups. Small groups are the inner workings of our fellowship, working together to fulfill the Great Commission of Jesus Christ on our campus. Each group has one mission: to love God and to love others by serving and investing in a particular community in CMU. This is done by following the example of the fellowship of Jesus' apostles, where each CoG forms a group seeking to edify each other through prayer, worship, and Scripture as we invite our community to join us in partaking in God's love for us.
